About Me

Passionate Wildlife Photographer & Conservation Advocate

Hi, I'm Amol Bais, a dedicated wildlife photographer with over a decade of experience capturing the raw beauty and essence of India's incredible biodiversity.

My journey began with a simple love for nature and has evolved into a mission to document and preserve the stories of our wild heritage. Through my lens, I aim to showcase the intricate relationships between wildlife and their habitats.

I specialize in wildlife photography, particularly focusing on the majestic big cats of Indian national parks, exotic bird species, and the diverse ecosystems that support them.
