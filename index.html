About The Photographer

Amol R. Bais

With over a decade of experience in wildlife photography, I've dedicated my life to capturing the raw beauty of nature and its magnificent creatures. My journey began in the forests of Central India, where I first encountered the majestic Bengal tiger, sparking a lifelong passion.

My work has been featured in several wildlife magazines and conservation campaigns. I believe photography is not just about capturing images, but about telling stories that inspire conservation and respect for our natural world.
