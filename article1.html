About Me

Hi, I'm Amol Bais, a passionate wildlife photographer with over a decade of experience capturing the beauty and essence of India's incredible biodiversity.

My journey began with a simple love for nature and has evolved into a mission to document and preserve the stories of our wild heritage. Through my lens, I aim to showcase the intricate relationships between wildlife and their habitats, hoping to inspire conservation efforts and deeper appreciation for our natural world.

I specialize in wildlife photography, particularly focusing on the big cats of Indian national parks, exotic bird species, and the diverse ecosystems that support them. My work has been featured in various wildlife magazines and conservation publications.

"Photography is not just about capturing an image; it's about telling a story that connects people with nature and ignites a passion for conservation."
